FAQ

What is your refund policy?

Unfortunately, our tickets are nonrefundable and nonresellable.
Our chiefs and vendors have traveled far and wide to help showcase their skills and ingredients! Our best suggestion is to mark your calendars when you buy your tickets so you don’t forget about this event!

What happens if there is inclement weather?

If it is raining, we will provide extra seating inside and cover our outdoor seating area. Incase of severe weather, we will notify everyone as soon as we can about the cancellation. We will only reimburse tickets in case of cancellation of bad weather.

Can I buy tickets at the venue?

Unfortunately, we will not be selling tickets at the venue. We will only be selling tickets online.

How does parking work?

Parking is offered at Republic Parking Garage (2 blocks from the venue). Please refer to the above section ‘Map + Directions’ for more details.

Can I bring outside food and drink?

Unfortunately, we do not allow outside food and drinks. You may bring empty waterbottles to fill them up at the venue. Be sure to come hungry because we will have plenty of food options for everyone!

Is there security on the premises?

There is security on the premise. We have asked the local Seattle Police Department to help keep everyone safe and having fun!
