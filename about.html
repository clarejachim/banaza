Our Origin

Bananza started when a group of friends got together
and shared their favorite meals with each other,
teaching and exploring new culinary cuisine and
expression. This eventually lead to a small scale annual
get together where friends invited friends who invited
friends. Eventually, it grew into the fest you know and love:
Bananza!

Bananza invites and collaborates with those who want to explore
their culinary pallets with tastes from all over the world. We invite
those distinguished in their culinary fields to join us for the
weekend and share their tasty bites with those who are curious!
