Our Mission

With humble beginnings of apartement and picnic blanket gatherings, BANANZA eventually grew into the festival you know and love today! BANANZA has taken place at the historic Pike Place Market since 2018 as an occasion for the city of Seattle to gather and for visitors to enjoy everything we have to offer.

We're Local & Global

We take pride in sourcing our ingredients locally, ensuring that produce is of the highest quality while supporting small businesses in Seattle. By showcasing a diverse culinary scene, the festival provides a platform for vendors in Pike Place Market to share their unique flavors and dishes. By promoting sustainable practices and supporting local businesses, BANANZA celebrates the rich food culture of Seattle and beyond.
